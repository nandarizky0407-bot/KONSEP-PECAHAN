Fabulous Fractions!

A magical journey through the world of fractions. Let's learn, play, and become a Fraction Master!

Trigger Question

Where do we use fractions?

Have you ever wondered why we need numbers like 1/2 or 3/4? Look around!

🍕

Sharing Pizza

If you cut a pizza into 8 slices and eat 3, you ate 3/8 of the pizza!

🍫

Chocolate Bars

A chocolate bar has 12 pieces. If you eat 4, you ate 4/12 (or 1/3) of the bar!

⏰

Telling Time

Half an hour is 1/2 of an hour. A quarter past is 1/4 of an hour!

💰

Shopping

"Half price" means you only pay 1/2 of the original cost. Fractions save money!

Part 2: Equivalent Fractions

What are Equivalent Fractions?

Equivalent fractions have the same value. They might look different, but they represent the same amount! You can find them by multiplying or dividing the top and bottom by the same number.
